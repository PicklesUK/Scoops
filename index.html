Welcome to Scoops!

Where every scoop tells a delicious story...


Allow us to set the scene. You’re walking around York, kids in tow. Ready to see the historic wonders of York you excitedly rush to Fenwick to explore the lavish and expansive history of the Vikings. But wait!

You feel a tug and look down to see your little angels begging for a sweet treat to satiate their thirst for adventure. You frantically look around Fenwick desperate to bring your child all the joy in the world. Then there it is. A quaint little hole in the wall called Scoops.

As you approach, you are greeted by a face that never fails to smile and could brighten up any room. Our ice cream is the remedy you’ve been looking for! With 25 years of ice cream genius under our belt we are able to proudly present to you our family-made expertly crafted flavours!

Wondering what our flavours might enjoy here at Scoops? Well worry not! We’re not some simple ice cream shop claiming to be amazing when our most adventurous flavours are mint choc chip. No hate to our mint fans of course!

We offer a variety of flavours that are always on rotation so you'll be sure to want to come back and see what you missed out on.
